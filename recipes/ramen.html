Ponyo's Ramen

Description

A simple yet tasty way to prepare ramen as seen in the movie Ponyo!

Ingredients

  • 2 Japanese instant ramen packs
  • 2 slices of ham or canadian bacon cut halfway down the middle
  • 1 soft boiled egg, cut in half
  • 1 green onion, chopped thinly

Instructions

  1. Prepare the ramen according to the package instructions and pour into a ramen bowl.
  2. Use the image below to help you layout the ham, egg, and green onions on top of the ramen.
  3. Enjoy!
